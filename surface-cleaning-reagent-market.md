Low pH reagents are often acidic, effectively removing mineral deposits and rust stains. Neutral pH reagents strike a balance between cleaning power and safety, commonly used for general surface cleaning. High pH reagents, on the other hand, are alkali-based and ideal for tackling tough grease and grime. Each type offers unique benefits and suitability for different cleaning applications.</p></p>
