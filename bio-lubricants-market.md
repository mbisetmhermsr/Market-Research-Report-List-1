<p><p>Bio-lubricants are environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional lubricants made from petroleum. There are two main types of bio-lubricants: plant-based and animal-based. Plant-based lubricants are derived from renewable sources like vegetable oils, while animal-based lubricants are made from animal fats. Both types offer similar benefits, such as biodegradability and reduced environmental impact. Plant-based lubricants are popular in industries like automotive and industrial machinery, while animal-based lubricants are often used in specialized applications like food processing.</p></p>
