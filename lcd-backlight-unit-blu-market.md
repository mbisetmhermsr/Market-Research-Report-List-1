<p><p>The LCD Backlight Unit (BLU) market is divided into two main types: Edge Type and Direct Type. Edge Type BLU is positioned around the edges of the display panel to provide uniform backlighting, while Direct Type BLU is positioned directly behind the panel for a more even distribution of light. Edge Type BLU is typically used in thinner displays, while Direct Type BLU is favored for larger screens where uniform lighting is crucial. These two market types cater to different display requirements and preferences.</p></p>
