<p><p>Carpet and carpet tile market types can be categorized into four main categories: woven, needle felt, knotted, and tufted. Woven carpets are made on a loom and are known for their durability and intricate designs. Needle felt carpets are made by interlocking fibers through a needle punch process, resulting in a dense and strong material. Knotted carpets have intricately woven patterns created by hand or machine. Tufted carpets are created by stitching yarn through a backing material, offering a versatile and cost-effective option for a variety of spaces.</p></p>
