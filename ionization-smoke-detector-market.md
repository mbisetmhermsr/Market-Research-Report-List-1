<p><p>Ionization smoke detectors are available in two main types: wireless smoke detectors and wired smoke detectors. </p><p>Wireless smoke detectors use radio frequency communication to send signals to a control panel or other interconnected detectors. They are easy to install and can be placed in hard-to-reach areas.</p><p>On the other hand, wired smoke detectors are connected by electrical wiring to a central control panel. They are reliable and require less maintenance compared to wireless detectors.</p><p>Both types of detectors are crucial for early fire detection and ensuring the safety of occupants.</p></p>
