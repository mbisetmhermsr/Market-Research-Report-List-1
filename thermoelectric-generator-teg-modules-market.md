<p><p>Thermoelectric Generator (TEG) Modules market consists of three main types: Multi-Stage, Single-Stage, and Thermocyclers. Multi-Stage TEG Modules are designed with multiple stages of thermoelectric materials to achieve higher efficiency in converting heat to electricity. Single-Stage TEG Modules have only one thermoelectric material layer and are simpler in design. Thermocyclers are specialized TEG Modules that utilize temperature fluctuations to generate electricity. Each type of TEG Module caters to different applications and requirements in the growing market for thermoelectric generators.</p></p>
