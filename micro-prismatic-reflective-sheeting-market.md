<p><p>Micro-prismatic reflective sheeting is commonly categorized into three main types based on the size of the prisms: Long-distance type, Short-distance type, and Full-prism type. Long-distance type reflective sheeting contains smaller prisms which allow for visibility from a greater distance, making it suitable for highways and large signs. Short-distance type sheeting has larger prisms for close-range visibility, making it ideal for urban areas and smaller signs. Full-prism type sheeting contains prisms on the entire surface, providing maximum reflectivity for various applications.</p></p>
