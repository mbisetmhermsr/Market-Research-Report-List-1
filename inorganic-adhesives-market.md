<p><p>Inorganic adhesives are classified into different types based on their composition and properties. Silicate type adhesives are known for their high strength and chemical resistance. Sulfate type adhesives offer good heat resistance and are often used in high temperature applications. Phosphate type adhesives are valued for their strong bonding capabilities. Borate type adhesives are known for their flame retardant properties. Other types of inorganic adhesives include aluminate and metasilicate adhesives, each with unique characteristics that make them suitable for specific applications in various industries.</p></p>
