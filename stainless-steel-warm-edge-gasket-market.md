<p><p>Stainless Steel Warm Edge Gaskets are available in two main types - single gasket and double sealing gasket. Single gaskets provide a single seal between the glass and the frame, offering basic insulation and preventing heat loss. On the other hand, double sealing gaskets feature an additional layer of sealing for enhanced thermal performance and improved energy efficiency. Both types of gaskets are crucial components in windows and doors, helping to maintain a comfortable indoor environment and reduce energy costs.</p></p>
