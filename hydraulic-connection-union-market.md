<p><p>The Hydraulic Connection Union market offers various types such as Welded Type, Card Sleeve Type, Withhold Type, and Flaring Type. Welded Type unions are permanently connected through welding, providing strong and leak-proof joints. Card Sleeve Type unions use a sleeve to secure connections without requiring special tools. Withhold Type unions feature a nut and gland that holds the connection in place. Flaring Type unions involve creating a tight seal by flaring the end of the pipe. Each type offers unique benefits depending on the specific hydraulic system requirements.</p></p>
