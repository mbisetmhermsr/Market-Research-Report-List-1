HPMC capsules are made from hydroxypropyl methylcellulose, widely used due to their vegetarian and vegan-friendly nature. Pullulan capsules are derived from tapioca starch and are known for their resistance to stomach acid. Sodium Alginate capsules are made from seaweed extract and are popular for their stability and slow dissolution. Other types of capsules include those made from starch, agar, and other natural ingredients.</p></p>
