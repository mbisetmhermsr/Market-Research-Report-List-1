<p><p>EV High Voltage Cables market consists of shielded and unshielded cables. Shielded cables are designed with an extra layer of insulation to protect signals from external interference, making them more reliable in high voltage environments. Unshielded cables, on the other hand, do not have the extra insulation layer and are commonly used in simpler applications where interference is not a concern.
