<p><p>The terminal tackle market is segmented into three main types: sleeve hook, round hook, and corner hook. Sleeve hooks feature a closed loop at the end, ensuring the line stays secure. Round hooks have a circular shape and are commonly used for general fishing purposes. Corner hooks have a sharp, angular bend that helps prevent fish from escaping. Each type caters to different fishing techniques and preferences, providing anglers with a variety of options to suit their needs and target species.</p></p>
