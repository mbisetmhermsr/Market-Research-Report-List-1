<p><p>Steel wire tire cord market types are categorized based on their tensile strength. Normal Tensile (NT) cords are commonly used in standard tires, while High Tensile (HT) cords offer increased strength for heavy-duty or high-performance tires. Super Tensile (ST) cords provide even higher tensile strength for specialized applications, and Ultra Tensile (UT) cords are designed for extreme conditions where maximum strength is required. Each type of cord offers varying levels of durability and performance to meet the specific needs of different tire applications.</p></p>
