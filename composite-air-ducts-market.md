<p><p>Composite air ducts are categorized into different types based on their material composition. Rubber plastic composite air ducts are made from a combination of rubber and plastic materials, providing flexibility and durability. Phenolic composite air ducts are composed of phenolic resin, offering high temperature resistance and fire retardant properties. Glass magnesium composite air ducts are made from a mix of glass fibers and magnesium oxide, providing strength and corrosion resistance. Each type of composite air duct offers unique benefits for various applications in HVAC systems.</p></p>
