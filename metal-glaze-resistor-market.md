<p><p>Metal Glaze Resistor Market consists of two main types: Thin Film Resistor and Thick Film Resistor. Thin Film Resistor is made by depositing a thin film of resistive material onto a ceramic substrate through a sputtering process, offering high precision and stability. On the other hand, Thick Film Resistor is created by screen printing a resistive paste onto a ceramic substrate and then firing it, providing a cost-effective solution with good power handling capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in various electronic applications.</p></p>
