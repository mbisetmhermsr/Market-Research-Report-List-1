<p><p>Industrial abrasives market is segmented into metallic abrasives and nonmetallic abrasives. Metallic abrasives are typically made of metal particles such as steel or aluminum and are used for cutting, grinding, and polishing hard surfaces like metal or stone. Nonmetallic abrasives include materials like sand, glass beads, and ceramics, which are used for softer materials like wood or plastic. Both types of abrasives play a crucial role in various industries for surface preparation, finishing, and shaping of materials.</p></p>
