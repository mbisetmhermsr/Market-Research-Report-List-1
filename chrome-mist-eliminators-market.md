<p><ul><li>Wire Mesh Defogger</li><li>Baffle Type Mist Eliminator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chrome mist eliminators are used in industrial processes to remove liquid droplets from gas streams. Two common types of mist eliminators are wire mesh defoggers and baffle type mist eliminators. Wire mesh defoggers use layers of knitted wire mesh to intercept droplets, while baffle type mist eliminators use a series of baffles to change the direction of flow and allow droplets to collect and drain. Both types are effective in reducing mist and improving the efficiency of industrial processes.</p></p>
