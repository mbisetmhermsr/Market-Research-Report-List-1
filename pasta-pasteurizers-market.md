<p><p>Pasta pasteurizers are used in the food industry to ensure the safety of pasta products by eliminating harmful bacteria through heat treatment. Two common types of pasta pasteurizers are compressor pasteurizers and water circulation pasteurizers. Compressor pasteurizers use compressed air to generate heat for pasteurization, while water circulation pasteurizers rely on circulating hot water to achieve the same goal. These different market types cater to varying production needs and preferences within the food industry.</p></p>
