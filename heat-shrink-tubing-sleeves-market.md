<p><p>Heat shrink tubing and sleeves come in various types, each designed for specific applications. PTFE tubing is commonly used in high-temperature and chemical-resistant environments, while FEP tubing offers similar properties but with a lower maximum temperature. PFA tubing is known for its high heat resistance and excellent dielectric properties, while ETFE tubing is ideal for applications requiring abrasion resistance. PET tubing is a cost-effective option for general-purpose insulation, while PEEK tubing provides exceptional mechanical performance. PTFE/FEP tubing combines the properties of both materials for versatile applications.</p></p>
