The types include carbon fiber, glass fiber, thermoplastics, aluminum material, and other materials. Carbon fiber panels are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, while glass fiber panels offer good impact resistance. Thermoplastic panels are lightweight and can be heated and reshaped. Aluminum panels are durable and corrosion-resistant. Other materials used in aviation honeycomb panels may include Kevlar or Nomex, each offering unique properties for specific aerospace applications.</p></p>
