<p><p>The Visible Light Sensor market includes various types such as Flash Effect Sensor and External Photoelectric Effect Sensor. Flash Effect Sensor detects light intensity changes quickly, commonly used in smartphone cameras to adjust flash settings. On the other hand, External Photoelectric Effect Sensor converts light energy into electrical signals externally, often used in industrial automation for detecting objects or measuring distances. Both types play a crucial role in applications requiring accurate light detection and measurement.</p></p>
