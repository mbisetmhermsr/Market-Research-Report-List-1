Safety harnesses are worn to prevent falls and distribute impact forces in case of a fall. Safety lanyards are used to connect the harness to an anchorage point. Anchorage connectors provide a secure attachment point. Fall arresters stop a fall in progress. Lifeline systems provide continuous protection while working at heights. Other equipment includes helmets, gloves, and safety footwear.</p></p>
