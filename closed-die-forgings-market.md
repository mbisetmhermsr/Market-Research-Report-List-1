<p><p>Closed die forgings market consists of three main types: Custom Forging, Captive Forging, and Catalog Forging. Custom Forging involves creating unique products tailored to specific customer requirements. Captive Forging refers to forging operations that are integrated within a company's own manufacturing facilities. Catalog Forging involves producing standard, off-the-shelf forged products that are readily available for purchase by customers. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the closed die forging market.</p></p>
