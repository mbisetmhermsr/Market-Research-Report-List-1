<p><ul><li>Simplex Pressure Reducing Sets</li><li>Duplex Pressure Reducing Sets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure reducing sets are used in various industries to reduce high pressure to a lower, more manageable level. Simplex pressure reducing sets consist of a single pressure reducing valve, while duplex pressure reducing sets have two valves for added redundancy and control. Simplex sets are suitable for smaller applications with relatively consistent pressure requirements, while duplex sets are used in larger systems that require continuous operation and backup in case of valve failure. Both types cater to different needs in the market for pressure control solutions.</p></p>
