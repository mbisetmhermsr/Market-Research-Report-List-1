<p><ul><li>Granules</li><li>Flour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic textured soy protein market consists of two main types: granules and flour. Granules are small pieces of soy protein with a coarse texture that can be used as a meat substitute or added to various dishes to enhance their texture and protein content. Flour, on the other hand, is a fine powder made from ground soy protein, which is typically used as a binder or thickener in food products such as veggie burgers, sausages, or meatballs. Both granules and flour offer a plant-based alternative to meat, making them popular choices among vegetarians and vegans.</p></p>
