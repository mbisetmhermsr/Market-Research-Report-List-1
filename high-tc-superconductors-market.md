<p><p>High-TC superconductors can be divided into two main market types: 1G HTS and 2G HTS. 1G HTS refers to first-generation high-temperature superconductors, which are made from ceramics like yttrium barium copper oxide. They are widely used in various industries for their high critical temperature and critical current density. On the other hand, 2G HTS are second-generation superconductors that offer even higher performance levels and improved efficiency, making them ideal for more demanding applications in energy, transportation, and medical sectors.</p></p>
