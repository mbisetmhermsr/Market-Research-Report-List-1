<p><p>A Moulded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB) is an electrical device designed to protect power distribution systems from overloads and short circuits. There are two main types of MCCBs: Thermal Magnetic MCCB and Electronic Trip MCCB. Thermal Magnetic MCCBs use both thermal (for sustained overloads) and magnetic (for short circuits) mechanisms to trip the breaker. Electronic Trip MCCBs use electronic devices to detect and trip the breaker in case of overloads or short circuits. Both types are widely used in various industries to ensure electrical safety and prevent damages in power systems.</p></p>
