<p><p>Semiconductor process valves market is segmented into various types including diaphragm valves, bellows valves, vacuum valves, ball valves, butterfly valves, gate valves, angle valves, Teflon valves, and others. Diaphragm valves provide better control and isolation, while bellows valves are used for high-purity applications. Vacuum valves are crucial for maintaining vacuum conditions. Ball valves offer quick shut-off capability. Butterfly valves are used for regulating flow. Gate valves are primarily used for on/off applications. Angle valves provide flow control in confined spaces. Teflon valves are resistant to corrosion and high temperatures. Other types of valves cater to specific process requirements in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
