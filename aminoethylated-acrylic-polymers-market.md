<p><ul><li>Water-reducible Type Aminoethylated Acrylic Polymers</li><li>Solvent Type Aminoethylated Acrylic Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aminoethylated Acrylic Polymers are divided into Water-reducible Type and Solvent Type. Water-reducible Type Aminoethylated Acrylic Polymers are mainly used in water-based formulations due to their ability to disperse in water, making them environment-friendly and easy to clean up. On the other hand, Solvent Type Aminoethylated Acrylic Polymers are used in solvent-based formulations where water is not compatible, providing excellent adhesion and durability. Both types cater to different market demands and applications within the polymer industry.</p></p>
