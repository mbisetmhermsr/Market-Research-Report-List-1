<p><p>Autotransfusion systems (ATS) are medical devices used to collect and reinfuse a patient's own blood during surgery. There are two main types of ATS on the market: Continuous Autotransfusion Systems and Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems. Continuous systems continuously collect and reinfuse blood throughout the surgery, while discontinuous systems collect blood during surgery and reinfuse it at the end. Both types help reduce the need for donor blood transfusions, decrease the risk of infection, and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
