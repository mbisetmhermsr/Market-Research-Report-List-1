There are three main types of oil filters on the market: Mechanical oil filters use a paper or synthetic filter media to trap debris. Magnetic oil filters utilize magnets to capture metal particles in the oil. Centrifugal oil filters spin oil at high speeds to separate contaminants. Each type has unique advantages and is designed to suit different applications and preferences.</p></p>
