<p><p>Electric tourist cars can be categorized into two main types based on the motor drive system: DC motor drive and AC motor drive. DC motor drive tourist cars are equipped with a direct current motor which provides a simple and cost-effective solution for low-speed applications. On the other hand, AC motor drive tourist cars utilize an alternating current motor that offers higher efficiency, better acceleration, and increased control options. Both types of market cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the electric tourist car industry.</p></p>
