<p><p>The aluminum frame market can be divided into three main types: exterior walls, curtain walls, and interior applications. Exterior walls refer to the use of aluminum frames in the construction of the outer walls of buildings, providing strength, durability, and aesthetic appeal. Curtain walls involve the use of aluminum frames to create a non-structural exterior envelope, typically made of glass panels. Interior market refers to the use of aluminum frames in interior applications such as partitions, doors, and windows to provide a sleek and modern look.</p></p>
