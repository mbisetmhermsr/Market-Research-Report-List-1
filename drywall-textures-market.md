<p><strong>The Drywall Textures Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Knockdown</li><li>Orange Peel</li><li>Popcorn</li><li>Sand Swirl</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drywall textures are a popular way to add visual interest to walls and ceilings. Knockdown texture involves applying joint compound to create a textured pattern that is then smoothed down. Orange peel texture creates a bumpy texture resembling the skin of an orange. Popcorn texture is a rough, bumpy texture that resembles popcorn kernels. Sand swirl texture involves creating swirl patterns using sand or a similar material. Other types of drywall textures include skip trowel, slap brush, and stomp texture. Each type offers a unique look and can be customized to suit different preferences.</p></p>
