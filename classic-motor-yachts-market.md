<p><p>Classic motor yachts can be classified into three main market types based on their hull design: Displacement hull, Planing hull, and Semi-displacement hull. Displacement hull yachts are designed for comfortable and stable cruising at lower speeds, ideal for long-distance journeys. Planing hull yachts are built for high speed and are more suitable for recreational activities. Semi-displacement hull yachts combine the characteristics of both displacement and planing hulls, offering a balance of speed and stability for a variety of boating experiences.</p></p>
