<p><p>Supercapacitor electrolytes can be classified into two main types: aqueous solution and organic solvent. Aqueous solution electrolytes are composed of water-based solutions, which offer high conductivity and low cost, making them suitable for low-power applications. On the other hand, organic solvent electrolytes contain non-aqueous solvents, providing higher energy density and better performance for high-power applications, but at a higher cost. Overall, the choice between these two types depends on the specific requirements of the supercapacitor application.</p></p>
