<p><p>Automotive exhaust gas sensors are sensors that monitor the exhaust gases emitted by vehicles. The main types of sensors are O2-Lambda Sensors, NOX Sensors, Particulate Matter Sensors, Differential Pressure Sensors, and Temperature Sensors. O2-Lambda Sensors measure oxygen levels in the exhaust gas, NOX Sensors detect nitrogen oxides, Particulate Matter Sensors monitor particulate emissions, Differential Pressure Sensors measure pressure imbalances, and Temperature Sensors monitor exhaust gas temperature. Other sensors in the market include MAP sensors, MAF sensors, and EGR sensors, which help optimize engine performance and reduce emissions.</p></p>
