<p><p>Resistive touch screen monitors are available in two main market types: single touch and multi touch. Single touch monitors allow users to input commands by pressing on the screen with one point of contact at a time. In contrast, multi touch monitors enable users to interact with the screen using multiple points of contact simultaneously, allowing for more complex gestures and commands. Both types of touch screen monitors are popular in various industries, with multi touch screens being more common in consumer electronics and interactive displays.</p></p>
