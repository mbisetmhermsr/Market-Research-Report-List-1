<p><p>Polybutylene Pipe Market Types include Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H is made up of a single type of monomer, resulting in a more uniform structure and better mechanical properties. On the other hand, PB-R contains a mixture of two different types of monomers, providing improved flexibility and impact resistance. Both types offer unique advantages and are used in various applications within the polybutylene pipe market.</p></p>
