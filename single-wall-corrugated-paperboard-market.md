<p><p>Single Wall Corrugated Paperboard is categorized into different types based on their thickness, which includes A(4.5~5.0mm), B(2.5~3.0mm), C(3.5~4.0mm), E(1.1~1.4mm), and Others market. A-grade paperboard is the thickest and most durable, suitable for heavy-duty packaging. B-grade is medium thickness and commonly used for shipping boxes. C-grade is slightly thinner and used for lighter products. E-grade is the thinnest and ideal for lightweight items. The "Others" market includes variations outside of these standard thickness ranges.</p></p>
