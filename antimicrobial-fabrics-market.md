<p><p>Antimicrobial fabrics are categorized into three main types - Synthetic Organic Compounds, Metal & Metallic Salts, and Bio-based materials. Synthetic Organic Compounds are chemically-created antimicrobials that are effective in killing bacteria and germs. Metal & Metallic Salts utilize metals like silver and copper, known for their antimicrobial properties. Bio-based materials are derived from natural sources, such as plants, and are considered eco-friendly alternatives to synthetic options. These different types of antimicrobial fabrics offer a wide range of choices for consumers looking for hygiene-enhancing textiles.</p></p>
