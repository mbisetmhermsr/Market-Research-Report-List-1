<p><p>Ground photovoltaic power stations can be categorized into two main types: crystalline silicon and thin film. Crystalline silicon photovoltaic power stations use silicon wafers to generate electricity, making up the majority of the market due to their high efficiency and reliability. Both technologies play a crucial role in expanding the solar energy market and reducing reliance on traditional energy sources.</p></p>
