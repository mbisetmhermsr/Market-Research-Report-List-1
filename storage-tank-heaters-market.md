<p><strong>The Storage Tank Heaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heat Tank Heaters</li><li>Direct Immersion Tank Heaters</li><li>Indirect Immersion Tank Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Storage tank heaters are used to maintain the temperature of stored liquids. There are three main types: direct heat tank heaters, which transfer heat directly to the liquid through contact; direct immersion tank heaters, which are submerged directly into the liquid to heat it; and indirect immersion tank heaters, which use a heat transfer fluid to indirectly heat the liquid. Each type is suited for different applications based on factors such as liquid viscosity, tank size, and desired temperature control.</p></p>
