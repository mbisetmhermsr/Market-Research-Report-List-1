<p><ul><li>Solid Slab</li><li>Hollow Slab</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precast concrete slabs are commonly available in two main market types: solid slab and hollow slab. Solid slabs are dense, thick panels that are used for heavy-duty applications where strength and durability are crucial. On the other hand, hollow slabs are lighter weight and have cavities running through them, making them ideal for projects where reducing weight and improving insulation are important factors. Both solid and hollow slabs offer a range of benefits in construction projects, including faster installation, reduced labor costs, and improved structural performance.</p></p>
