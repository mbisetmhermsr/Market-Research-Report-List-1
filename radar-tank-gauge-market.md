<p><p>Radar tank gauge market is divided into two main types: non-contact systems and contact systems. Non-contact systems use radar technology to measure liquid levels in tanks from a distance, making them ideal for hazardous or hard-to-reach environments. Contact systems, on the other hand, require physical contact with the liquid in the tank for accurate measurements. Both types have their own advantages and limitations, catering to different industries and applications in the market.</p></p>
