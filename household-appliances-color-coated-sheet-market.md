<p><p>Household Appliances Color-Coated Sheet Market is divided into two main types- PCM (Pre-Coated Metal) and VCM (Laminated Coated Metal). PCM sheets have a pre-painted surface that provides durability and scratch resistance, making them ideal for appliances like refrigerators and washing machines. On the other hand, VCM sheets have a PVC film laminated onto the metal surface, offering a wide range of colors and patterns for a more decorative finish. Both PCM and VCM sheets are popular choices in the household appliances industry for their aesthetic appeal and functional benefits.</p></p>
