The market for treating this condition is categorized into various types. Anti-vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) drugs are used to inhibit the growth of new blood vessels and reduce swelling. Corticosteroids help reduce inflammation and edema. Anticoagulants prevent blood clot formation. Fibrinolytics dissolve blood clots. Other treatments may include laser therapy or surgical procedures to restore blood flow and preserve vision.</p></p>
