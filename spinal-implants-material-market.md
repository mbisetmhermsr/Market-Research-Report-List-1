<p><p>Spinal implants are divided into different types based on their function in the market. Fusion implants are used to join two or more vertebrae together, while fixation implants stabilize the spine after surgical procedures. VCF implants are designed to treat vertebral compression fractures, while decompression implants help relieve pressure on the spinal cord. Motion preservation implants are used to maintain flexibility and movement in the spine. Each type of implant serves a specific purpose in treating various spinal conditions.</p></p>
