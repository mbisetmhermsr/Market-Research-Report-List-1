<p><p>Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) cells are divided into two main types based on the doping of the silicon material used in the cell structure: P-Type and N-Type. P-Type cells have a positive charge carrier concentration, while N-Type cells have a negative charge carrier concentration. Both types have a tunnel oxide layer that passivates the contact surface, improving the cell's efficiency and performance. The choice between P-Type and N-Type cells depends on the specific requirements of the solar cell application.</p></p>
