<p><p>Biopharma buffer market types can be categorized into four main groups: Phosphates Type, Acetates Type, TRIS Type, and Others. Phosphates Type buffers are commonly used due to their ability to maintain a stable pH range. Acetates Type buffers are known for their buffering capacity at a slightly acidic pH. TRIS Type buffers offer good stability and solubility. The Others category includes various buffer types like citrates, carbonates, and MES. Each type of buffer has unique characteristics that make them suitable for different applications in the biopharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
