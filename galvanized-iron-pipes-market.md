<p><strong>The Galvanized Iron Pipes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Seamless</li><li>Welded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized iron pipes are divided into two main types based on manufacturing process - seamless and welded. Seamless pipes are made by extruding a solid billet through a die to create a hollow tube without any seams. Welded pipes are made by rolling a flat plate into a tubular shape and then welding the edges together. Both types have their advantages and are commonly used in various industries such as construction, plumbing, and infrastructure development.</p></p>
