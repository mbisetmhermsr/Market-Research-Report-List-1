<p><strong>The Blue Hydrogen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Methane Reforming</li><li>Auto Thermal Reforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blue Hydrogen is produced using steam methane reforming (SMR) or auto thermal reforming (ATR) processes. SMR involves combining natural gas with steam and heat to produce hydrogen and carbon dioxide. ATR is a similar process that does not require the addition of steam, using only heat to break down natural gas into hydrogen and carbon dioxide. Both SMR and ATR are prevalent in the blue hydrogen market, providing different options for hydrogen production with varying efficiency and environmental impacts.</p></p>
