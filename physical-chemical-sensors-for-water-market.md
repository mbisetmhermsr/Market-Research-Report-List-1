<p><p>Physical and Chemical sensors for water can be categorized into two main market types: Mono Parameter Sensor and Multi-Parameter Sensor. </p><p>Mono Parameter Sensors are designed to measure a single specific aspect of water quality, such as pH levels or temperature. These sensors are typically more affordable and easier to use, making them ideal for basic water monitoring applications.</p><p>On the other hand, Multi-Parameter Sensors are capable of measuring multiple parameters simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive analysis of water quality. These sensors are often used in more advanced applications where a broader range of data is needed for accurate monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
