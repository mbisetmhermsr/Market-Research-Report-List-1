<p><strong>The Submarine Pipeline Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ERW Pipes</li><li>LSAW Pipes</li><li>SSAW Pipes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Submarine pipeline market consists of three main types of pipes: ERW (Electric Resistance Welded) pipes, LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, and SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes. ERW pipes are formed by cold forming steel sheets, while LSAW pipes are manufactured by bending and welding steel plates. SSAW pipes are made by forming a spiral shape and welding the edges together. Each type of pipe offers different advantages in terms of construction cost, durability, and efficiency for underwater pipeline projects.</p></p>
