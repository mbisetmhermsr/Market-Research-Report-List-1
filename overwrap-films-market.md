<p><p>Overwrap films can be categorized into two main types: coated and uncoated films. Coated overwrap films are treated with a layer of coating material, such as wax or plastic, to provide additional protection and sealing properties. Uncoated overwrap films, on the other hand, do not have any additional coating and are typically used for lighter packaging applications. Both types of films serve a similar purpose of protecting and securing products during transportation and storage.</p></p>
