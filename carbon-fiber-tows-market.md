<p><ul><li>PAN Based Carbon Fiber</li><li>Pitch Based Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Tows market is segmented into two types: PAN Based Carbon Fiber and Pitch Based Carbon Fiber. PAN Based Carbon Fiber is produced from polyacrylonitrile and is known for its high strength, stiffness, and excellent fatigue resistance. On the other hand, Pitch Based Carbon Fiber is derived from petroleum or coal tar pitch and is characterized by its high thermal conductivity and chemical resistance. Both types of carbon fibers are widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and sports equipment due to their superior performance characteristics.</p></p>
