<p><p>Dental glass powders can be categorized into two main types based on their properties and reactions: Inert glass powders and Reactive glass powders. Inert glass powders do not chemically react with other materials and are primarily used for their aesthetic properties in dental applications. On the other hand, Reactive glass powders have chemical reactivity with other substances, allowing for bonding and adhesion in dental restorative materials. Both types play crucial roles in the dental industry, offering a variety of options for dental professionals.</p></p>
