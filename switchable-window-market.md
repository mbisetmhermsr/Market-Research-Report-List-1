<p><strong>The Switchable Window Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switchable window technology has evolved into three main types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass allows users to adjust the tint of the window electronically. SPD Smart Glass uses suspended particle devices to control the transparency of the glass. PDLC Smart Glass utilizes polymer dispersed liquid crystal technology to switch between opaque and transparent states. These technologies provide users with control over privacy, sunlight exposure, and energy efficiency in buildings.</p></p>
