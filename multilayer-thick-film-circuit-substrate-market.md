<p><p>Multilayer thick film circuit substrates are used in various electronic applications. Alumina substrates are ceramic-based substrates known for their high thermal conductivity and excellent electrical insulation properties. They are commonly used in high-power and high-frequency applications. On the other hand, AlN substrates are made from aluminum nitride and offer even higher thermal conductivity, making them suitable for applications requiring superior heat dissipation. Both types of substrates play a crucial role in enabling the performance and reliability of electronic devices.</p></p>
