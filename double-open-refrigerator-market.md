<p><strong>The Double-Open Refrigerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-cooled</li><li>Air-cooled</li><li>Mixed Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct-cooled refrigerators use a cooling coil connected to the refrigeration unit placed inside the cabinet. Air-cooled refrigerators utilize a fan to circulate cold air throughout the cabinet. Mixed refrigeration combines both direct-cooled and air-cooled systems for efficient cooling. Each type caters to different consumer needs and preferences. Direct-cooled refrigerators are known for their energy efficiency, while air-cooled refrigerators are popular for their consistent temperature control. Mixed refrigeration offers a balance between the two, providing optimal cooling performance.</p></p>
