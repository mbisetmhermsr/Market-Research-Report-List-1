<p><strong>The Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Bags Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Film Strength Method</li><li>Tubular Quench Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Bags market can be categorized into two types based on the manufacturing method: Flat Film Strength Method and Tubular Quench Method. Flat Film Strength Method involves stretching the film in two directions simultaneously to enhance its strength and durability. On the other hand, Tubular Quench Method involves blowing a tube of molten polypropylene, which is then biaxially stretched to improve its properties. Both methods produce high-quality BOPP bags with different characteristics to cater to various packaging needs.</p></p>
