<p><p>Polystyrene (PS) Resins market consists of two main types: General Purpose Polystyrene (GPPS) and High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS). GPPS is a versatile, transparent plastic commonly used in packaging, food containers, and household goods. On the other hand, HIPS is a more durable and impact-resistant form of polystyrene, suitable for applications requiring increased toughness like electronics packaging and construction materials.
