<p><p>Pre-insulated forms in the automotive connector terminals market refer to terminals that come with an insulating covering, which helps protect the connection from environmental factors and prevents short circuits. On the other hand, non-insulated forms do not have an insulating covering and may require additional insulation to protect the connection. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, with pre-insulated forms offering convenience and protection out of the box, while non-insulated forms may provide more flexibility for custom applications.</p></p>
