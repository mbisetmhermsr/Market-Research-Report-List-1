<p><p>Huntington's Disease Therapeutics Market includes various types of drugs such as Antidopaminergics, Anticonvulsants, Antipsychotics, and Antidepressants. Antidopaminergics target dopamine receptors to help manage symptoms of Huntington's Disease. Anticonvulsants are prescribed to reduce seizures and improve muscle control. Antipsychotics are used to treat psychiatric symptoms like hallucinations and delusions. Antidepressants are often prescribed to help manage mood disorders associated with Huntington's Disease. These different types of drugs aim to alleviate symptoms and improve the quality of life for patients with Huntington's Disease.</p></p>
