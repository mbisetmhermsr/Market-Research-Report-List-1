<p><p>Low frequency coupling capacitors are commonly used in various electronic circuits such as oscillation circuits, timing circuits, delay circuits, and filter circuits. In oscillation circuits, coupling capacitors help stabilize the frequency of the circuit. Timing circuits use these capacitors to control the duration of signals. Delay circuits rely on coupling capacitors to create specific time delays. Additionally, in filter circuits, coupling capacitors are utilized to block DC signals while allowing AC signals to pass through, ensuring proper filtering of the desired frequencies.</p></p>
