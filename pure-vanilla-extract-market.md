<p><p>Pure Vanilla Extract is available in two main market types: liquid and powder forms. Liquid vanilla extract is made by soaking vanilla beans in alcohol and is commonly used in baking and cooking. Powdered vanilla extract is made by drying liquid vanilla extract, resulting in a concentrated, easy-to-store form of vanilla. While both forms can be used interchangeably in recipes, powdered vanilla extract is often preferred for its long shelf life and easy storage. Both types offer the rich, aromatic flavor of pure vanilla in various culinary applications.</p></p>
