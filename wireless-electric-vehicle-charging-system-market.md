<p><strong>The Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging</li><li>Dynamic Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging refers to the installation of fixed charging pads or charging stations in designated locations such as homes, parking lots, or public charging stations. This system works by transferring electric power from the charging pad to the vehicle through electromagnetic induction.</p><p>Dynamic Wireless Electric Vehicle Charging, on the other hand, involves charging the vehicle while it is in motion. This technology enables continuous charging of electric vehicles on roads or highways using wireless power transfer systems embedded in the road surface. This allows for longer driving ranges and eliminates the need to stop for charging.</p></p>
