<p><p>Hermetically sealed sliding doors are available in two main types: single leaf and double leaf. Single leaf doors consist of a single sliding panel, providing access to smaller openings, while double leaf doors have two sliding panels that open from the center, allowing for wider entrances. Both types are designed to create an airtight seal when closed, ensuring maximum protection against external elements such as dust, germs, and moisture. These doors are commonly used in hospitals, laboratories, clean rooms, and other environments requiring strict hygiene control.</p></p>
