<p><p>Viscose sponges are primarily categorized into two main types- Rayon Sponge and Synthetic Sponge. Rayon sponges are made from cellulose fibers derived from wood pulp, resulting in an absorbent and soft texture. On the other hand, Synthetic sponges are manufactured from man-made materials such as polyester or nylon, offering durability and scrubbing power. Both types are commonly used in various cleaning applications due to their effectiveness in absorbing liquids and scrubbing surfaces.</p></p>
