Laser Navigation Type AGVs use lasers to scan and map the environment for navigation. Magnetic Navigation Type AGVs follow magnetic strips or tapes embedded in the floor for guidance. Each type offers unique advantages and is suited for different environments and applications within the market.</p></p>
