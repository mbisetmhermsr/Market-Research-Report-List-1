<p><p>Substrate Like-PCB (SLP) technology offers two main types of PCB substrates: 30μm/30μm and 14μm/14μm. The 30μm/30μm SLP market is characterized by a thicker copper foil, providing higher current-carrying capacity and improved signal integrity. On the other hand, the 14μm/14μm SLP market offers a thinner copper foil for reduced impedance and higher frequency capabilities. Both types cater to different applications and requirements in the electronics industry, providing flexibility and performance benefits for various design needs.</p></p>
