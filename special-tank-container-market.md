<p><p>Special tank containers have various types including reefer/heated tank containers, super insulated tanks, and others. Reefer/heated tank containers are equipped with refrigeration or heating systems to maintain specific temperatures for temperature-sensitive cargo. Super insulated tanks have extra insulation for transporting temperature-sensitive goods. Other types of special tank containers may include cryogenic tanks for transporting liquefied gases, lined tanks for corrosive materials, or swap body tanks for intermodal transport. Each type serves specific purposes in the transportation of special cargo.</p></p>
