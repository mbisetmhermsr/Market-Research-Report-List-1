<p><p>Inorganic grease market includes two types: Bentonite Grease and Silicone Grease. Bentonite Grease is made from bentonite clay and is widely used in high temperature and heavy load applications due to its excellent thermal stability and resistance to water washout. Silicone Grease, on the other hand, is formulated with silicone oil and is known for its wide temperature range, chemical resistance, and electrical insulating properties. Both types of inorganic grease offer unique characteristics and are commonly used in various industrial applications.</p></p>
