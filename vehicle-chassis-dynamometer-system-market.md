<p><ul><li>Multi Roller</li><li>Single Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle Chassis Dynamometer System market can be categorized into two main types: Multi Roller and Single Roller. The Multi Roller system utilizes multiple rollers to simulate real-world driving conditions on a vehicle's chassis. This type of system is commonly used for testing heavy duty vehicles or vehicles with higher power output. On the other hand, the Single Roller system uses only one roller for testing vehicles with lower power output or for basic performance testing. Both types of systems are essential for evaluating the performance and emissions of vehicles.</p></p>
