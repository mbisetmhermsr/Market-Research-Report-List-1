<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Optical Patch Cord</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optical Patch Cord</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber patch cord market consists of two main types: single-mode and multimode fiber optic patch cords. Single-mode patch cords are designed to carry only one mode of light, typically used for long-distance communication. They have a smaller core diameter and provide higher transmission speeds. On the other hand, multimode patch cords can carry multiple modes of light simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances. They have a larger core diameter but offer lower transmission speeds compared to single-mode patch cords.</p></p>
