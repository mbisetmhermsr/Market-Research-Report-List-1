<p><ul><li>Single-band</li><li>Dual-band</li><li>Tri-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VoIP routers come in different market types based on their frequency bands. Single-band routers operate on only one frequency band, usually 2.4GHz. Dual-band routers operate on both 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands, providing more flexibility and less interference. Tri-band routers operate on three bands - one 2.4GHz and two 5GHz bands - allowing for even faster speeds and better performance in crowded networks. Each market type offers different features and benefits to users depending on their specific needs and network requirements.</p></p>
