<p><ul><li>Translational</li><li>Pointer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Translational sprinkler trucks are designed for moving in a straight line while spraying water, making them ideal for covering large, open areas efficiently. Pointer sprinkler trucks, on the other hand, are equipped with a rotating turret-like mechanism on top that can be pointed in different directions, allowing for more precision and flexibility in watering specific areas. Both types of sprinkler trucks are commonly used in agriculture, landscaping, and firefighting applications.</p></p>
