<p><p>Rubber Bonded Abrasives refer to abrasive products where abrasive grains are bonded together by rubber. There are two main types of Rubber Bonded Abrasives: Natural Rubber Bonded Abrasives and Synthetic Rubber Bonded Abrasives. Natural Rubber Bonded Abrasives are made from raw rubber while Synthetic Rubber Bonded Abrasives are manufactured using synthetic rubber compounds. Both types offer flexibility, durability, and resistance to heat and wear, making them ideal for various grinding and polishing applications in industries such as metalworking, woodworking, and automotive.</p></p>
