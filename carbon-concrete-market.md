<p><p>Normal Carbon Concrete refers to traditional concrete mixtures that contain a medium to high amount of carbon content. These mixtures are commonly used in construction due to their strength and durability. On the other hand, Ultra Low Carbon Concrete is a newer type of concrete that contains significantly lower carbon content, achieved through the use of alternative materials such as fly ash or slag. Ultra Low Carbon Concrete is gaining popularity due to its environmental benefits and potential for reducing carbon emissions in the construction industry.</p></p>
