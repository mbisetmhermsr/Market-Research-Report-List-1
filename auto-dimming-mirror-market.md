<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Electrodynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Auto-dimming mirrors are available in two main types: mechanical and electrodynamic. Mechanical auto-dimming mirrors use a simple system of photo sensors and electrochromic gel to automatically adjust the mirror's tint based on the amount of light detected. On the other hand, electrodynamic auto-dimming mirrors utilize a more advanced technology that involves applying an electrical charge to the mirror's surface to change its tint. Both types of auto-dimming mirrors provide a convenient solution for reducing glare and improving visibility while driving.</p></p>
