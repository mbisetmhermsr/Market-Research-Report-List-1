<p><p>Soft Touch Films are available in three main market types: Water Based, Solvent Based, and FDA Compliant. Water Based Soft Touch Films are environmentally friendly and offer excellent adhesion to various substrates. Solvent Based Soft Touch Films provide a more durable finish and are suitable for applications requiring resistance to chemical exposure. FDA Compliant Soft Touch Coatings are specifically designed for use in food packaging and medical devices, ensuring safety and regulatory compliance. Each market type offers unique benefits based on specific usage requirements.</p></p>
