<p><p>The truck steering system market consists of three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering, Electric Power Steering, and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering. Hydraulic Power Steering uses hydraulic pressure to assist the driver in steering the truck. Electric Power Steering relies on an electric motor to provide steering assistance, while Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering combines both hydraulic and electric systems for improved steering control. These systems are designed to enhance maneuverability and responsiveness in trucks of varying sizes and applications.</p></p>
