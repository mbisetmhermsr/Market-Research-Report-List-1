<p><p>Static Transfer Systems (STS) are devices that provide seamless power transfer between two power sources to ensure uninterrupted power supply. The STS market can be categorized into three types: switch, cabinet, and others. Switch STS are standalone devices that can be easily installed in existing power systems. Cabinet STS are larger systems housed in cabinets for centralized power distribution. The "others" category includes custom and specialized STS solutions designed for specific applications. Each type of STS serves the same function of ensuring reliable power transfer.</p></p>
