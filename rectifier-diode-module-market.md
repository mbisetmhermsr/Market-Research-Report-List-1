<p><p>Rectifier diode modules are categorized into different types based on their specific characteristics and applications. Ordinary rectifier diode modules are commonly used for general purpose rectification. Fast recovery diode modules are designed for high-speed switching applications, providing faster recovery times. Schottky diode modules offer low forward voltage drop and fast switching capabilities. Other types of rectifier diode modules may include specialized configurations for unique requirements, such as high voltage or temperature environments. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries and electronic devices.</p></p>
