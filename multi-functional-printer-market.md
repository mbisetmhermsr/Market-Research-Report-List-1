<p><p>The multi-functional printer (MFP) market consists of two main types: color multi-function printers (CMFP) and monochrome multi-function printers (MMFP). CMFPs can print, copy, fax, and scan documents in vivid color, making them suitable for businesses or individuals requiring high-quality graphics or images. On the other hand, MMFPs can perform the same functions as CMFPs, except they only produce black and white prints, which are more cost-effective for users who primarily deal with text-based documents. Both types cater to diverse needs and preferences, offering a range of productivity features in a single device.</p></p>
