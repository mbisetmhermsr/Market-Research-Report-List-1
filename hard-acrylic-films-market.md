<p><p>Hard acrylic films are used for various applications, and they are available in two types: Single-Side Coated and Double-Side Coated. In Single-Side Coated films, only one side of the film is coated with acrylic material, providing a glossy finish and enhanced durability. On the other hand, Double-Side Coated films have both sides coated with acrylic material, offering excellent protection and a uniform appearance. Both types of films are widely used in industries such as packaging, printing, and labeling for their versatility and strength.</p></p>
