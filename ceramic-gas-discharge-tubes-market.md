<p><strong>The Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters</li><li>Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Gas Discharge Tubes are used in electrical systems for protection against overvoltage. Surface Mount Gas Tube Arresters are small, compact devices that can be easily mounted on circuit boards, ideal for applications where space is limited. Through Hole Gas Discharge Tubes are larger devices that are soldered directly onto the circuit board, providing robust overvoltage protection. Both types of gas discharge tubes function by ionizing gas to create a low-resistance path for excess voltage to be safely diverted.</p></p>
