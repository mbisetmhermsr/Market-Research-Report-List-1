<p><p>Soft CMP pads are used in the semiconductor industry for chemical mechanical polishing of wafers. There are two main types of Soft CMP pads in the market - Abrasive Type and Normal Type. Abrasive Type pads contain abrasive particles for more aggressive material removal, while Normal Type pads are smoother and less aggressive. Abrasive Type pads are preferred for hard materials, while Normal Type pads are used for softer materials. Both types of pads are essential in providing a smooth and polished surface for semiconductor production.</p></p>
