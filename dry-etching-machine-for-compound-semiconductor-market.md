<p><p>Dry etching machines for compound semiconductor market can be classified into two main types: physical etching and chemical etching. Physical etching involves using reactive ions or plasma to remove material from the surface of the semiconductor wafer, while chemical etching relies on the chemical reaction between the etchant and the material to achieve the etching process. Both methods have their advantages and are widely used in the manufacturing of compound semiconductor devices for various applications in electronics and photonics industries.</p></p>
