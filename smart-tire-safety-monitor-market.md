<p><p>The Smart Tire Safety Monitor market consists of two main types: Interior Monitor and External Monitor. Interior Monitors are installed inside the vehicle to provide real-time data on tire pressure and temperature to the driver. External Monitors, on the other hand, are mounted on the exterior of the vehicle to continuously monitor tire conditions and alert the driver of any potential issues. Both types of monitors aim to enhance road safety and prevent accidents by ensuring that tires are properly inflated and maintained.</p></p>
