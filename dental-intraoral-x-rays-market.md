<p><p>Dental intraoral X-rays market consists of three main types - Bitewing X-rays, Periapical X-rays, and Others. Bitewing X-rays are used to detect early signs of tooth decay between teeth, while Periapical X-rays focus on capturing the entire tooth from crown to root to assess the root and surrounding bone. Other types may include occlusal X-rays for detecting issues with the floor of the mouth or cone beam computed tomography for obtaining more detailed 3D images of the teeth and surrounding structures.</p></p>
