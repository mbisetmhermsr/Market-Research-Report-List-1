<p><p>Schottky Rectifier Diode market consists of two main types: Dual Common Anode and Dual Common Cathode. In Dual Common Anode configuration, two diodes share a common anode terminal, allowing them to conduct current simultaneously in opposite directions. On the other hand, in Dual Common Cathode setup, the diodes share a common cathode terminal, enabling them to conduct current in the same direction simultaneously. These configurations offer flexibility and efficiency in various electronic applications requiring rectification and voltage regulation.</p></p>
