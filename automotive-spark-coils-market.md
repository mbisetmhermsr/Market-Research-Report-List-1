<p><ul><li>Single-Spark</li><li>Multi-Spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Spark Coils come in two main types: Single-Spark and Multi-Spark. Single-Spark coils generate one spark per ignition cycle, producing a consistent and reliable ignition source. On the other hand, Multi-Spark coils are designed to produce multiple sparks in rapid succession, providing a more intense and efficient ignition process. Single-Spark coils are typically used in standard automotive engines, while Multi-Spark coils are popular in high-performance vehicles to improve combustion efficiency and engine power.</p></p>
