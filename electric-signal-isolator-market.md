<p><strong>The Electric Signal Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Isolation</li><li>Electromagnetic Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric signal isolators provide electrical separation between input and output circuits to prevent ground loops and voltage spikes from damaging equipment. Optical isolation uses light-emitting diodes and photodiodes to transmit signals across an isolation barrier, while electromagnetic isolation uses transformers or capacitors to transfer signals without direct electrical connection. Both types of isolators are crucial for industrial applications where noise and interference can affect the performance of sensitive equipment, ensuring reliable signal transmission and protecting electronic components.</p></p>
