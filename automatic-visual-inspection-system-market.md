<p><p>Front inspection and back inspection are two types of automatic visual inspection systems used in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics. Front inspection systems are designed to detect defects or inconsistencies on the front surface of the product, ensuring quality and performance standards are met. Back inspection systems, on the other hand, focus on identifying issues on the reverse side of the product, ensuring overall quality control and product reliability. Both systems play a crucial role in reducing defects and improving production efficiency.</p></p>
