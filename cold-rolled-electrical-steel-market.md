<p><strong>The Cold Rolled Electrical Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Electrical Steel</li><li>Non-oriented Electrical Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold Rolled Electrical Steel market includes two main types: Oriented Electrical Steel and Non-oriented Electrical Steel. Oriented Electrical Steel is designed for specific use in transformers, motors, and generators, with its grains oriented in a single direction to enhance magnetic properties. Non-oriented Electrical Steel, on the other hand, has random grain orientation and is used in a wide range of electrical applications. Both types are essential in electrical equipment manufacturing due to their high magnetic permeability and low core losses.</p></p>
