<p><p>Fluorinated Polyimide Market Types include Perfluoropolymer Type and Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type. Perfluoropolymer Type is a type of fluorinated polyimide that contains a complete fluorine content and provides properties like high thermal resistance, chemical resistance, and low surface energy. Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type is another fluorinated polyimide type with lower fluorine content, offering a balance between desirable characteristics such as flexibility, adhesion, and improved processability. Both types cater to different applications based on specific requirements and preferences in various industries including electronics, aerospace, and automotive.</p></p>
