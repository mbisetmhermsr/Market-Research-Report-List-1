<p><strong>The Liquid Leak Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Invasive Leak Detection</li><li>Non-Invasive Leak Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid Leak Sensors market includes two main types: Invasive Leak Detection and Non-Invasive Leak Detection. </p><p>Invasive Leak Detection systems involve sensors that come in direct contact with the liquid, such as probes or cables, to detect leaks in real-time. These systems are commonly used in areas where maintenance access is available.</p><p>On the other hand, Non-Invasive Leak Detection systems use technologies like acoustic, thermal, or optical sensors to detect leaks without direct contact with the liquid. These systems are ideal for situations where contacting the liquid is not feasible or safe.</p></p>
