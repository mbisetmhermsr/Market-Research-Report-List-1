<p><strong>The Resin Additives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fillers</li><li>Colorants</li><li>Plasticizers</li><li>Antioxidants</li><li>Stabilizers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Resin additives can be categorized into various types based on their specific functions in enhancing the properties of the resin. Fillers are substances that improve the strength and durability of the resin. Colorants are used to add color or pigmentation to the resin. Plasticizers help increase the flexibility and workability of the resin. Antioxidants and stabilizers are added to prevent the resin from degrading over time. Other types of additives may include flame retardants, UV stabilizers, and rheology modifiers to further customize the properties of the resin.</p></p>
