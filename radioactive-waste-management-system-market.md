<p><p>Radioactive waste management systems are categorized into three types based on the level of radioactivity: Low Level Waste (LLW), Intermediate Level Waste (ILW), and High Level Waste (HLW). LLW includes items like contaminated clothing and tools, ILW consists of reactor components and resins, and HLW comprises highly radioactive materials from fuel reprocessing. Each type requires specific treatment and disposal methods to minimize environmental and health risks. The market for these waste management systems is driven by regulations and the increasing demand for safe disposal solutions.</p></p>
