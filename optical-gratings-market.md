<p><ul><li>Reflective Gratings</li><li>Transmission Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical gratings are a key component in various optical devices. Reflective gratings are designed to reflect light at specific angles and are commonly used in spectroscopy and laser systems. On the other hand, transmission gratings allow light to pass through, diffracting the light in a specific direction. They are often used in applications such as telecommunications and astronomy. Both types of gratings play crucial roles in manipulating light waves for numerous applications in the optical industry.</p></p>
