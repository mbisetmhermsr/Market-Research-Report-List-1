<p><p>The Heavy Soda Ash market can be classified into two types based on production methods - Solvay Method and Trona Method. Solvay Method involves the chemical reaction of sodium chloride and limestone to produce soda ash. Trona Method, on the other hand, relies on the mining and processing of trona ore, a naturally occurring mineral rich in sodium carbonate. Both methods have their own advantages and applications in various industries, contributing to the overall diversity and stability of the Heavy Soda Ash market.</p></p>
