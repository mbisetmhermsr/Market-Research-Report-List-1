<p><p>Chip assembly and testing market consists of various segments including wafer probing, final test, and other markets. Final test involves testing the fully packaged and assembled chip before it is shipped to customers. The "other" market segment includes services such as burn-in testing, system-level testing, and reliability testing. These segments collectively ensure the quality and functionality of semiconductor chips before they are released to the market.</p></p>
