<p><p>Agricultural chemical colorants market primarily consists of two types - dyes and pigments. Dyes are water-soluble substances that provide color to agricultural chemicals by forming a chemical bond with the substrates. On the other hand, pigments are insoluble particles that impart color through physical absorption or reflection of light. Both types of colorants play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetic appeal of agricultural chemicals and helping in their identification and application.</p></p>
