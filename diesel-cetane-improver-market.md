Peroxides act as catalysts in the cetane ignition process, leading to a more complete combustion and improved engine performance. The "Others" category includes various additives like antioxidants and stabilizers that enhance the cetane rating of diesel fuel. Each type offers unique benefits for optimizing diesel engine performance.</p></p>
