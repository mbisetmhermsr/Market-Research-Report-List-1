<p><strong>The Water Coolers and Dispensers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are two main types of water coolers and dispensers in the market: Bottled Water Cooler (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) coolers. BWCs require water bottles to be manually loaded onto the cooler for dispensing. On the other hand, POU coolers are directly connected to the main water supply and filter the water internally before dispensing. BWCs are more common in residential settings, while POU coolers are often found in commercial buildings due to their continuous supply of filtered water.</p></p>
