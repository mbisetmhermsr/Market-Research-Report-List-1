<p><ul><li>Thermal Transfer Printing</li><li>Digital Inkjet Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal transfer printing involves transferring a design from a thermal transfer ribbon onto a textile using heat, making it suitable for light-colored fabrics. Digital inkjet printing, on the other hand, involves spraying dye directly onto the fabric through tiny nozzles, allowing for intricate designs and vibrant colors on both light and dark fabrics. Both methods are popular in the Industrial High-Speed Textile Digital Printing Machine market, catering to different needs and preferences of manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
