<p><ul><li>ADS-B In</li><li>ADS-B Out</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ADS-B In refers to the market for receivers and equipment that can receive signals from other aircraft's ADS-B Out transmissions. This allows pilots to see nearby traffic and receive important information such as weather updates. ADS-B Out refers to the market for transmitters that broadcast the aircraft's own position and information to other aircraft and air traffic control. Both ADS-B In and ADS-B Out are essential components of the ADS-B system, which is becoming increasingly mandatory for aircraft around the world.</p></p>
