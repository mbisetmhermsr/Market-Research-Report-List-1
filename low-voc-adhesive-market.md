<p><p>Low VOC adhesives are classified into different types based on their composition and application. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and commonly used in paper, packaging, and woodworking industries. Hot-melt adhesives are solid at room temperature and are ideal for bonding different materials quickly. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to cure and are often used in automotive and construction industries. Other adhesives include pressure-sensitive adhesives, which provide strong bonds without heat or solvent activation.</p></p>
