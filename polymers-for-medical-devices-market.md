<p><p>Polymers for medical devices market can be categorized into different types such as PVC, PP, PS, PE, TPE, and others. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is widely used due to its flexibility and cost-effectiveness. PP (polypropylene) is known for its durability and chemical resistance. PS (polystyrene) is lightweight and transparent, making it suitable for disposable medical products. PE (polyethylene) is commonly used for packaging medical devices. TPE (thermoplastic elastomer) combines the properties of rubber and plastic, providing flexibility and impact resistance. Other polymers offer unique characteristics for specific medical device applications.</p></p>
