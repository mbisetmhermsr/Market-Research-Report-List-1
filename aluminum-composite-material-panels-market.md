<p><p>Aluminum Composite Material Panels, also known as ACP, come in different types, with the two most common being PVDF Coated ACP and PE Coated ACP. PVDF Coated ACP panels are known for their superior durability and weather resistance, making them ideal for outdoor applications. On the other hand, PE Coated ACP panels are more cost-effective and are commonly used for interior projects. Both types offer a wide range of colors and finishes, providing flexibility for various design needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
