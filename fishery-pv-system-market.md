<p><p>The Fishery-PV System market includes two main types: Floating Type and Fixed Type. The Floating Type system consists of solar panels installed on water bodies such as ponds or lakes, enabling fish to thrive in the shaded areas beneath the panels. In contrast, the Fixed Type system involves mounting solar panels on land near fishing sites, providing energy for equipment and facilities. Both market types offer sustainable energy solutions for fisheries while promoting environmental conservation and resource efficiency.</p></p>
