<p><p>Cryogenic insulation films come in two types based on the coating applied to them: single side coated and double side coated. Single side coated films have insulation material applied on only one side, providing thermal protection in one direction. On the other hand, double side coated films have insulation material on both sides, offering enhanced thermal protection in both directions. These variations in coating types cater to different requirements for insulation in cryogenic applications.</p></p>
