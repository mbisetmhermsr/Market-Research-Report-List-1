<p><p>Silicon-Infiltrated Silicon Carbide (SiSiC) is available in two main forms in the market: powder and blocky. SiSiC powder is finely ground material that is commonly used for coating applications and as an additive in various industries. On the other hand, blocky SiSiC is a solid, block-shaped material that is often used in the manufacturing of components and parts with high strength and wear resistance requirements. Both forms of SiSiC offer superior mechanical properties, thermal stability, and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
