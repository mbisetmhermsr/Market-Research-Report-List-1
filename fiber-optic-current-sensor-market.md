<p><p>Fiber-optic current sensors are classified into two main types: DC type and AC type. DC type sensors are used for measuring direct current, while AC type sensors are used for measuring alternating current. DC type sensors are preferred for applications where high accuracy and stability are required, such as in power distribution systems. On the other hand, AC type sensors are suitable for applications where rapid and dynamic current measurements are needed, such as in power grid monitoring. Both types of sensors offer reliable and accurate current measurement solutions for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
