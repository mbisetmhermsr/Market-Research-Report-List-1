<p><p>Abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA) repair devices are used to treat the condition where the main blood vessel in the abdomen expands and weakens. Synthetic graft systems are one type of device used, involving the insertion of a synthetic tube to replace the weakened area. Endovascular stent graft systems are another type, which involve using a fabric tube supported by a metal stent to reinforce the weakened area. Both types are minimally invasive procedures that help prevent the rupture or further expansion of the aneurysm, reducing the risk of a life-threatening condition.</p></p>
