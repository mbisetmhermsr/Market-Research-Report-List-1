<p><strong>The Velvet Lamination Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal Velvet Film</li><li>Wet Velvet Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Velvet lamination films come in two main types: thermal velvet film and wet velvet film. Thermal velvet film is designed to be used with thermal laminating machines and provides a smooth and luxurious finish to printed materials. Wet velvet film, on the other hand, requires a wet adhesive to be applied before lamination and offers a velvet-like texture and appearance. Both types of velvet lamination films are popular choices for enhancing the visual appeal and tactile experience of printed products.</p></p>
