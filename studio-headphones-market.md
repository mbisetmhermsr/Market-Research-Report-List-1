<p><p>Studio headphones come in three main market types: Closed Back, Semi-Open Back, and Fully-Open Back. Closed Back headphones provide noise isolation and are ideal for tracking and recording in a studio environment. Semi-Open Back headphones offer a balanced sound with some leakage, suitable for mixing and monitoring. Fully-Open Back headphones provide a natural sound, but may not be ideal for recording due to sound leakage. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences of studio professionals and enthusiasts.</p></p>
