<p><p>Insect Growth Regulator Bait market consists of three main types: Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors, Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics, and Anti-Juvenile Hormone Agents. Chitin Synthesis Inhibitors disrupt the formation of insect exoskeletons, while Juvenile Hormone Analogs & Mimics mimic the effects of juvenile hormones to prevent proper development. Anti-Juvenile Hormone Agents block the action of juvenile hormones, leading to disrupted growth and development in insects. These different types of insect growth regulators are used in pest control to target specific stages of insect development.</p></p>
