<p><strong>The Manual Dental Micro-sandblasters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2-tank</li><li>1-tank</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual dental micro-sandblasters are versatile tools used in dental procedures for cleaning and surface preparation. There are three market types available: 2-tank, 1-tank, and other. </p><p>In a 2-tank micro-sandblaster, there are two separate tanks. One tank holds the abrasive material, such as aluminum oxide, while the other holds the air supply. This allows for better control over the blasting pressure and ensures a consistent flow of abrasive material.</p><p>In a 1-tank micro-sandblaster, there is only one tank which combines both the abrasive material and the air supply. This design is more compact and simpler to operate.</p><p>The "other" market refers to any micro-sandblasters that do not fit into the 2-tank or 1-tank categories. These may include variations in design, functionality, or features that cater to specific dental applications.</p></p>
