<p><p>Wood anticorrosive coatings are used to protect wood surfaces from corrosion and degradation. The market for these coatings can be categorized into two main types: solvent-borne coatings and water-borne coatings. Solvent-borne coatings contain volatile organic compounds that evaporate during the drying process, while water-borne coatings use water as the main solvent. Both types of coatings provide excellent protection against corrosion and are commonly used in the construction industry for outdoor wood structures such as decks, fences, and outdoor furniture.</p></p>
