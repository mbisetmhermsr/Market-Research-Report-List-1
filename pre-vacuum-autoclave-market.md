<p><p>Pre-vacuum autoclaves are classified into two main types based on their orientation: horizontal autoclaves and vertical autoclaves. Horizontal autoclaves are designed with a chamber that is oriented horizontally, allowing for easy loading and unloading of materials. These autoclaves are commonly used in laboratory settings where space is limited. Vertical autoclaves, on the other hand, have a chamber that is oriented vertically, making them ideal for facilities with limited floor space. They are often used in medical and dental settings for sterilizing instruments and equipment.</p></p>
