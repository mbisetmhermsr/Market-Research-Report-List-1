<p><ul><li>Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H)</li><li>Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polybutene-1 (PB) pipes market is segmented into two main types - Polybutene-1 Homopolymer (PB-H) and Polybutene-1 Random Copolymer (PB-R). PB-H pipes are made of pure Polybutene-1 material, offering high strength and durability. On the other hand, PB-R pipes contain a mixture of Polybutene-1 and other copolymers, providing enhanced flexibility and impact resistance. Both types are commonly used in plumbing and heating systems due to their corrosion resistance, ease of installation, and long-term performance benefits.</p></p>
