<p><p>Mist lubrication oils are divided into two main types - non-strain type and anti-wear type. Non-strain type oils are designed to lubricate machinery parts without causing any strain, reducing friction and wear. On the other hand, anti-wear type oils are specifically formulated to protect metal surfaces from wear and tear, extending the lifespan of the machinery. Both types of oils play a crucial role in minimizing friction and ensuring the smooth functioning of machines in various industries.</p></p>
