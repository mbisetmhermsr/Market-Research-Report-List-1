<p><p>Alkalinity control chemicals market consists of two main types - acids and bases. Acids are used to lower the alkalinity levels in water by neutralizing the excess alkalis. On the other hand, bases are utilized to increase alkalinity levels by neutralizing excess acids. These chemicals play a crucial role in maintaining the pH balance in various industries such as water treatment, agriculture, and manufacturing. Both acids and bases are essential components in regulating alkalinity levels to ensure optimal performance and efficiency in industrial processes.</p></p>
