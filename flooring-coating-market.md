<p><ul><li>Water-Based Flooring Coating</li><li>Solvent-Based Flooring Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flooring coating markets can be divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based flooring coatings are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and have low levels of volatile organic compounds. On the other hand, solvent-based coatings are more durable, provide better adhesion to surfaces, and have faster drying times. Both types have specific advantages and are suitable for different applications, depending on factors such as environmental concerns, durability, and application requirements.</p></p>
