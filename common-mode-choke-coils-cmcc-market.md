<p><strong>The Common Mode Choke Coils (CMCC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Common Mode Choke Coils</li><li>Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SMD Common Mode Choke Coils are surface mount devices designed for printed circuit boards, offering smaller size and higher efficiency compared to Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils. On the other hand, Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils are mounted by inserting their leads through holes on the PCB, providing higher mechanical stability and durability. Both types are utilized in electronic circuits to suppress electromagnetic interference and noise in signal transmission. The choice between SMD and Through Hole depends on space constraints, performance requirements, and production processes.</p></p>
