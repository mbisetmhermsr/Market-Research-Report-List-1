<p><p>Membrane cleaning chemicals market includes various types such as caustic, oxidants or disinfectants, acids, surfactants, and chelating agents. Caustic chemicals are used for breaking down organic matter, while oxidants or disinfectants help to remove microorganisms. Acids are effective in dissolving inorganic scales, surfactants help to improve the wetting of membrane surfaces, and chelating agents are used to bind metal ions. Each type of chemical plays a crucial role in cleaning and maintaining membranes used in various industrial processes.</p></p>
