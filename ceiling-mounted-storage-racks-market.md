<p><p>Ceiling mounted storage racks come in two main types: liftable and non-liftable. Liftable racks are designed to be raised and lowered to easily access items stored overhead. These racks are typically operated manually or with the help of a motorized system. On the other hand, non-liftable racks are stationary and do not have the capability to be adjusted in height. Both types of racks are commonly used in garages, warehouses, and other storage spaces to maximize vertical storage capacity.</p></p>
