<p><p>Rigid copper busbars are categorized into three main types based on the purity of copper used in their production. ETP type busbars are made from electrolytic tough pitch copper which has high conductivity. OF type busbars are made from oxygen-free copper, which is purer and offers improved conductivity. Other types of busbars may include a combination of copper alloys or coatings for specific applications. Each type of rigid copper busbar has unique characteristics and is suitable for different electrical installations.</p></p>
