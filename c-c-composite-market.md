<p><ul><li>Chemical vapor deposition</li><li>Liquid impregnation process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical vapor deposition and liquid impregnation process are two types of composite market technologies. Chemical vapor deposition involves coating a substrate with a thin film by introducing gases into a reaction chamber. This method is commonly used in the production of advanced materials such as ceramic and metal coatings. On the other hand, the liquid impregnation process involves saturating a porous material with a liquid resin to enhance its mechanical properties. Both methods are crucial in the manufacturing of high-performance composite materials.</p></p>
