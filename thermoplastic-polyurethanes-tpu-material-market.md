<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile materials used in various industries due to their excellent properties such as flexibility, durability, and chemical resistance. There are three main types of TPU materials based on their chemical composition: Polyester-based TPUs, Polyether-based TPUs, and Polycaprolactone TPUs. Polyester-based TPUs offer high mechanical strength and abrasion resistance, while Polyether-based TPUs provide superior flexibility and low-temperature resistance. Polycaprolactone TPUs are known for their biodegradability and soft touch, making them suitable for applications requiring these properties.</p></p>
