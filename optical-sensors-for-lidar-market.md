<p><p>Optical sensors for LiDAR can be categorized into two main types: Non-Scanning LiDAR and Scanning LiDAR. Non-Scanning LiDAR systems utilize a fixed sensor to continuously collect data in all directions, offering a wide field of view but lower resolution. In contrast, Scanning LiDAR systems use a rotating or moving sensor to cover a specific area with higher precision and resolution. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications such as autonomous vehicles, environmental monitoring, and industrial mapping.</p></p>
