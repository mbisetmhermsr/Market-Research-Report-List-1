<p><ul><li>DOT4</li><li>DOT5</li><li>DOT5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Racing Brake Fluid comes in various types, including DOT4, DOT5, and DOT5.1. DOT4 brake fluid is one of the most commonly used types and is suitable for most vehicles. DOT5 brake fluid is silicone-based and is preferred for high-performance racing vehicles due to its superior resistance to high temperatures. DOT5.1 brake fluid is a mix between DOT3 and DOT4, offering a higher boiling point than DOT3 fluid. Each type of brake fluid has specific qualities that cater to different needs and preferences in the racing industry.</p></p>
